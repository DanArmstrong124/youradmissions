Youradmissions.org.uk offer services to schools who are their own admissions authority, such as Foundation, Trust and Voluntary Aided Schools, Academies and Free Schools.
We offer a full appeals service which includes paperwork submissions, case preparation and representation at appeal hearings on behalf of the school/governing body
The appeals service we provide is done in a way that ensures that the case of the admissions authority is presented in the best way possible, we remain in constant contact with the school to ensure that all information is provided for each case and no stone is left unturned.
With vast experience of all areas of school admissions and an understanding of the importance of complying with code and policy, we ensure that flexibility is used with a common sense approach working with efficiency and accuracy to ensure that the correct outcome is reached as quickly as possible.
